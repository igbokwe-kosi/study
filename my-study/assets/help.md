# Help

## Navigating the Study

For each section and question:

1. **Read carefully** - Take time to understand the question or prompt
2. **Provide your answer** - Select your response or enter the information requested
3. **Click Next** - Once you've answered, click the **Next** button to proceed to the next question
4. **Take your time** - There's no time limit; work at your own pace

## If You Need to Stop

You may **withdraw at any time** without penalty. Simply close the browser or notify the researcher conducting the study.

## Technical Tips

- **Maximize your browser window** for the best viewing experience
- Ensure your screen brightness is adequate for viewing visualizations
- If you experience any technical issues, please contact: contact@revisit.dev

## Questions?

If you have questions about the study or any specific question, please ask the researcher or contact us at contact@revisit.dev.
