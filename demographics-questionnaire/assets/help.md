# Help & FAQ

## General Questions

### How long will this take?

The questionnaire typically takes 10-15 minutes to complete.

### Do I have to answer all questions?

Most questions are required for the study, but some are optional. Optional questions are clearly marked as such.

### Can I save my progress and come back later?

You can navigate between sections using the progress bar, but you must complete the entire questionnaire in one session.

### Is my information secure?

Yes. All data is encrypted and stored securely on protected servers. Only authorized research staff have access to your information.

## Specific Fields

### Date of Birth

Please enter your date of birth in MM/DD/YYYY format (e.g., 01/15/1980).

### Phone Number

Enter your phone number with area code in the format (XXX) XXX-XXXX.

### Race/Ethnicity

You may select multiple races if you identify as multiracial. Ethnicity (Hispanic/Latino status) and Race are separate questions.

### Employment Status

Select the option that best describes your current situation. If you are retired but also working part-time, select "Employed Part-Time."

### Insurance Information

You can select multiple types of insurance if you have more than one coverage.
