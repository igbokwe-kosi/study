# Patient Demographic Questionnaire

## Welcome

Thank you for participating in this study. We appreciate you taking the time to complete this demographic questionnaire.

## Purpose

This questionnaire collects demographic and diabetes-related information to help researchers understand how patients track and review their health data. Your responses will be used for research purposes only.

## What to Expect

This questionnaire includes three short sections:

1. **Personal Information** – Gender identity, age group, and highest level of education
2. **Health Demographics** – Diabetes-related health status and glucose data practices
3. **Review Frequency** – How often you review your glucose data or reports

## Privacy & Confidentiality

All responses are anonymous and no identifying information is collected. All data collected will be stored securely and only authorized research personnel will have access to it.

## Your Rights

Your participation is entirely voluntary. You may skip any question you are not comfortable answering or withdraw from the study at any time without penalty.

Please proceed to the first section when you are ready.
