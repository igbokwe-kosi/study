# Mini VLAT - Visualization Literacy Assessment Test

## Welcome

Thank you for participating in this visualization literacy assessment. This test evaluates your understanding of data visualizations and visual perception.

## Test Overview

This assessment contains **12 multiple-choice questions** about diverse data visualizations.

**Duration:** Approximately 10-15 minutes

## How It Works

For each question, you will:

1. See a **data visualization** on the right side of the screen
2. Read a **problem or question** on the left side
3. Choose the **BEST answer** from the provided options
4. If unsure, you can select **'Skip'** to move to the next question

## Tips for Success

- **Maximize your browser window** for optimal viewing of visualizations
- Take your time to carefully examine each visualization
- Read the question thoroughly before selecting an answer
- Your honest responses are valuable to us

## What to Expect

The visualizations will cover various types including:

- Bar charts and column charts
- Line graphs and area charts
- Scatter plots
- Pie charts
- Heat maps
- And other common visualization types

Your responses will help us understand how effectively different visualization designs communicate data.
