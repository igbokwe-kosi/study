# Informed Consent

## Study Purpose

This study aims to assess your ability to understand and interpret data visualizations. The results will help improve visualization design practices and education.

## What You're Agreeing To

By participating in this study, you agree to:

- Complete a 12-question visualization literacy assessment
- Allow your responses to be used for research purposes
- Provide feedback about your experience (optional)

## Your Data & Privacy

- Your responses are **confidential and anonymous**
- Data is stored securely and only accessible to authorized researchers
- Results will be used for research and education purposes only
- You will not be identified in any publications or presentations

## Your Rights

- **Voluntary Participation:** Your participation is entirely voluntary
- **Right to Withdraw:** You can withdraw at any time without penalty
- **Right to Skip:** You may skip any question if you're uncomfortable answering
- **No Compensation Penalty:** Withdrawing will not affect any compensation you receive

## Questions or Concerns

If you have any questions about this study or your rights as a participant, please contact us at contact@revisit.dev.
