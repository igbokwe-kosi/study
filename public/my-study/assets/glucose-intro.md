# **Glucose Data Visualization Assessment**

In this section, you will complete the main study task by reviewing visualizations of 7 days of glucose tracking data presented at different levels of aggregation.

You will see glucose readings displayed in five chart variations, each with a different number of data points per day:

* **1 point per day:** One average glucose value per day  
* **2 points per day:** Two average glucose values per day  
* **4 points per day:** Four average glucose values per day  
* **24 points per day:** Twenty-four average glucose values per day

After reviewing each visualization, you will be asked questions about how clear and useful you found the display. At the end, you will also compare all five chart variations side by side and select your preferred option. Your feedback will help us understand which sampling levels and data aggregations are most effective for presenting glucose information to patients during clinical consultations.  
Please take your time reviewing each chart and provide your honest assessment. This section should take approximately 3-4 minutes.
