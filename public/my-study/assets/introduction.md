# **About This Study**

## **Welcome**

Thank you for your interest in participating in this research study conducted at the University of Calgary.

## **Study Purpose**

This study investigates how different ways of summarizing glucose data may influence people’s trust in data visualizations and preferences for making decisions.

## **What This Study Involves**

Your participation will include three parts:

### **Part 1: Demographic Questionnaire**

We'll ask you about:

* Basic demographic information  
* Your educational background  
* Your experience with data and visualizations

### **Part 2: Visualization Literacy Assessment Test**

You will answer a set of questions about various types of data visualizations. This helps us understand visualization literacy, or how people read and interpret visual information.

### **Part 3: Data Visualization Survey**

In the central part of the study, you will view glucose data displayed at different aggregation levels and answer questions about your trust in them and your preferences.

## **Time Required**

This study will take approximately **30 minutes** to complete.

## **Your Rights as a Participant**

* **Voluntary Participation:** Your participation is completely voluntary  
* **Right to Withdraw:** You may withdraw at any time without giving a reason and without penalty  
* **Right to Skip Questions:** You may skip any question you're uncomfortable answering  
* **Confidentiality:** Your responses are confidential and will be kept secure
